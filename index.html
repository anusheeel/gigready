For gigging cover musicians

Know what to practice before Friday's gig.

GigReady turns your songs, setlists, confidence ratings, rehearsal notes, and shared music links into one focused practice queue.

See what it does Get support

The wedge

A setlist app built around readiness, not clutter.

Add your repertoire, build upcoming gigs, mark each song red, yellow, or ready, and let GigReady surface the songs that need attention before showtime.

Practice queue

Rank songs by upcoming gig, confidence, and last-practiced date so rehearsal time has a clear target.

Setlists and gigs

Create gig cards, order songs, and see which setlists each song belongs to from the Songs tab.

Stage notes

Keep key, capo, BPM, start cues, arrangement notes, transitions, and reference links close at hand.

Share to add

Build your song list from the apps you already use.

Share a track or link from Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, Amazon Music, Safari, or Notes. GigReady opens a review screen, then saves the song to your library or a selected gig.

No lyrics catalog

GigReady does not provide copyrighted lyrics, sheet music, recordings, or backing tracks. Users add their own notes and reference links.

Private by default

No account is required. Songs, gigs, readiness, and notes are stored locally on device.
