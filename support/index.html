What GigReady Does

GigReady helps gigging musicians and bandleaders organize songs, gigs, setlists, practice readiness, and stage notes.

Do I need an account?

No. GigReady works without creating an account.

Does GigReady provide lyrics or recordings?

No. GigReady does not provide copyrighted lyrics, sheet music, recordings, or backing tracks. Users add their own performance notes and reference links.

Where is my data stored?

Your songs, gigs, setlists, readiness ratings, and notes are stored locally on your device. The share extension uses the same local app group storage so shared links can appear in the main app.

How do I share a song into GigReady?

From apps such as Spotify, YouTube, Apple Music, Amazon Music, Safari, or Notes, use the iOS Share button and choose GigReady. Review the song title, artist, and reference URL, then tap Save.
