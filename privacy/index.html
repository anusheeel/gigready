Privacy Policy

GigReady is designed to keep your gig and practice information private and local to your device.

Information You Enter

GigReady stores the songs, gigs, setlists, readiness ratings, notes, and reference links that you add to the app. This information is stored locally on your device and in the app group container used by the GigReady share extension.

Share Extension

When you share a link or text from another app to GigReady, GigReady uses that shared content to prefill a song draft. You can review and edit the information before saving it.

Data Collection

GigReady does not collect personal data for tracking. GigReady does not sell your data.

Copyrighted Content

GigReady does not provide copyrighted lyrics, recordings, or sheet music. Users are responsible for the notes and links they add.

Contact

For support or privacy questions, contact support@gigready.xyz.
